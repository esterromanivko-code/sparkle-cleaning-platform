Simple Process

Booked in Three Steps

No phone calls. No haggling. Post your job, let cleaners compete for your business, and pick the best fit.

1
Post Your Job

Describe your space, cleaning type, and preferred date. Takes two minutes and costs nothing to post. Your job goes live instantly.

2
Receive Bids

Verified cleaners send competitive quotes directly to you. Read real reviews, see ratings, compare prices — then decide on your timeline.

3
Book & Relax

Choose your cleaner, confirm the booking, and step back. Payment is held securely and only released when you're fully satisfied.

Our Standard

Why Seattle Chooses Sparkle

🛡

Every cleaner passes a full criminal background check and government ID verification before their first booking. Renewed annually.

⭐
Verified Reviews Only

Every rating comes from a real, completed booking. No fake reviews, no star-padding — just honest feedback from real clients.

💰
Transparent Pricing

You see every bid before you commit. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices. The price you accept is exactly the price you pay.

Insured & Bonded

Seattle Focused
